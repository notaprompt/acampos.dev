28 years in business, all verbal quotes, losing hot leads because quoting took too long. Orellana Landscaping LLC in Woodbridge, VA. The problem wasn't the price - it was the paper.

Mobile-first PWA. Enter the job details, get a professional bilingual (English/Spanish) proposal PDF in under 60 seconds. Share via text, email, or WhatsApp on the way out of the driveway. Every proposal saved becomes data for the pricing calculator layer coming next - patterns across past quotes surfacing what a job should cost based on type, size, and season.

Hosted free. Single shared login. Duplicate any past proposal to re-quote a repeat client in two taps.

The QuickBooks invoice was the baseline. This is what comes before it.
