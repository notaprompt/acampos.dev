How it works

Capture once. Review the batch. Send it all.

The repeating work bills itself into a batch; a person reviews the whole thing on one screen and sends it with a single confirm. Nothing goes out without that confirm.

1

Capture

Recurring jobs are templated; one-off jobs are captured by text from the field — once, at the source.

2

Review

The month's invoices are generated as a batch. Anything unclear is held aside for a human, never billed blind.

3

Send all

One confirmation sends the whole batch through QuickBooks. Idempotent and resumable — never a double-send.

Built to be trusted

Your books. Your control. Always.

Orellana Tools is a careful layer on top of QuickBooks — it never replaces it, and it never acts behind your back.

  • ✓Confirm-before-send. Every invoice and payment is previewed; nothing posts without your explicit confirmation.
  • ✓Never auto-sends. A wrong amount can't go out under your brand — the batch you review is the batch that sends.
  • ✓It's your QuickBooks. We read and write your existing books over Intuit's official API. Nothing new to migrate.
  • ✓Tenant isolation. One company can never reach another's data — enforced by design.
