Built it because I got tired of dictation tools that phone home. Floating desktop widget — zipped lips idle on screen. Click to record, ear listens. Click to stop, whisper transcribes locally. Text auto-copies to clipboard. Lips come back.

Audio never saved. No cloud. No account. No telemetry. 48kHz → whisper.cpp on-device. The mic is yours.

macOS only. Requires Apple Silicon. Right-click → Open on first launch.
