Floating desktop widget. Zipped lips idle on screen. Click to record — ear listens. Click to stop — whisper transcribes locally. Text auto-copies to clipboard. Lips come back.

Audio never saved. No cloud. No account. 48kHz → whisper.cpp on-device.

macOS only. Requires Apple Silicon. Right-click → Open on first launch.
